Vacation Timer App

Track your vacation countdown with ease!

Features

  • Countdown Timer: Set a date and time for your vacation, and the app will countdown until the specified time.
  • Customizable Vacation Name: Name your vacation for a personalized touch.
  • Beep Alert: A beeping sound plays when the timer hits zero.
  • Hide/Show Timer: Minimize the app or show the timer with just a click.

How to Use the Vacation Timer

Follow these simple steps to use the Vacation Timer:

  1. Enter a name for your vacation.
  2. Select the date and time when your vacation begins.
  3. Click the "Start Timer" button to start the countdown.
  4. When the timer reaches zero, you'll hear a beep sound and see a pop-up notification!
